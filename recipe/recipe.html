Cabbage with Dried Beancurd Puff

  1. Put sesame oil into frying pan
  2. Put cabbage and stir fry until cabbage softens
  3. Put some dried beancurd puff
  4. Add 1 tablespoon of light soy sauce and salt
  5. Ready to serve

Tomato with Red Onion

  1. Put sesame oil
  2. Add red onion and some salt
  3. Fry the red onion until it softens
  4. Put tomato cubes and some water
  5. Add 1 tablespoon of light soy sauce and salt
  6. Ready to serve

Steamed Egg with Wolfberries

  1. Crack 6 eggs and mix well
  2. Add 1 tablespoon of light soy sauce and some water. Mix well with eggs
  3. Spread sesame oil onto metal plate
  4. Pour the mixed eggs gently onto the metal plate
  5. Put some wolfberries on top of the eggs
  6. Steam for 10 minutes before serving
